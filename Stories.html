Experiencing Chairlift

The Chairlift is a unique experience of having one’s legs dangling in the air.

Kind of similar to enjoying waves striking just at the ankles at the beach. 

However, here you experience light breezes instead.

It is a live bird-eye view seeing the tips of the tall trees and sitting alongside your friend or 

loved one as you soar across the sky like Aladdin on your not so magical carpet…

But,

Of pure joy indeed!
