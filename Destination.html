Destination Guide: Neelum Valley

About Neelum Vallley

Neelum Valley is a stunning and picturesque region located in the Azad Jammu and Kashmir region of Pakistan.
It is known for its breathtaking natural beauty, lush landscapes, and serene environment.
Neelum Valley is a true gem of Pakistan, offering a unique blend of natural beauty, cultural richness, and adventure opportunities. However, it's important to plan your visit carefully and stay informed about any travel restrictions or safety concerns, especially given the region's geopolitical situation.
